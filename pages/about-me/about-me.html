Melanie Nesteruk

Currently working as a software developer at RoviSys in the Boston, Massachusetts area. Graduated from Kent State University with a degree in Computer science and a degree in Visual Journalism. Yep, I know that's a strange combination, but it's what I wanted to do. My goal is to find a way to combine my communication and technological skills and create something great.

But I know I love to tell stories and I love to create and I want to make something incredible. And somewhere in that, I know there is a place for a storytelling software developer or a passionate tech-saavy journalist.
